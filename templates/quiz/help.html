About

C++ Quiz is written by Anders Schau Knatten. If you discover any errors, or would like to contribute, please contact me at anders AT knatten.org.

Help/FAQ

What is this?

This is a simple online quiz that you can use to test your knowledge of the C++ programming language.

Which version of the standard is assumed?

Unless otherwise specified, C++11.

I don't agree with an answer!

Contact me (see above) and I'll check it out.

What features are planned?

In order of decreasing likelihood

  1. More questions in the database
  2. Possibility to answer "doesn't compile", "undefined behaviour" or "unspecified behaviour"
  3. A possibility to register and keep your score
  4. A "race" mode where you can race one or more of your friends

How can I help?

If you know C++, by adding more questions. If you know Python/Django/JavaScript, by extending functionality. If you know web-design, by improving the design. Please contact me at anders AT knatten.org.
